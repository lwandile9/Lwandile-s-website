About me

Hi, I'm Lwandile Toto, a passionate software developer and tech enthusiast with a love for solving problems and empowering others through technology. With experience across various tech stacks including PHP, JavaScript, Java, and MySQL, I have worked on diverse personal projects ranging from web and mobile applications to the system testing. Currently, I’m honing my skills as a software development intern at CAPACITI, where I’m expanding my knowledge in HTML, CSS, Javascript, Java, Python, React, and Node.js. I’m also dedicated to building a community through xhosadev.com, a platform that helps young people succeed in tech by providing valuable resources, mentorship, and entry-level job opportunities. My goal is to inspire the next generation of developers, especially from underrepresented backgrounds, by sharing my journey and offering them the tools to build a successful career in technology.
